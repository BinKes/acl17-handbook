Abstract#==#This study aims to predict clinical depression, a prevalent mental disorder,
from
blog posts written in Japanese by using machine learning approaches. The study
focuses on how data quality and various types of linguistic features
(characters, tokens, and lemmas) affect prediction outcome. Depression
prediction achieved
95.5% accuracy using selected lemmas as features.
